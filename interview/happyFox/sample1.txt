I applied online. The process took 1 week.I interviewed at HappyFox (Chennai) in 12/9/2023

Interview

The interview consisted of four rounds. 
The first round was a telephonic interview with HR. 
After passing this round, I was invited to their office for the second round, which was a written aptitude test. 
This test covered technical aptitude questions, quantitative reasoning, and verbal questions. 
The third round was a technical interview, and the final round was an interview with the CEO.
